Last updated: February 25, 2024

Shipping

All orders for our products are processed and shipped out in 1-3 business days. We will always endevour to have orders shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays though this may not always be possible.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders or postal service disruptions, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery.

If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, we will contact you via email.
